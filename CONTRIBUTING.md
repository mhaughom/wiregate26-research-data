# Contributing and scientific review

Corrections, alternative models and attempts to falsify the interpretation are
welcome. Open an issue describing the affected file, release or commit, the
method used, and enough code or calculations to reproduce the finding.

Please distinguish among:

1. errors in the copied source coordinates;
2. errors in extraction or coordinate conversion;
3. modelling choices or numerical implementation;
4. interpretation of what could have caused the recorded change; and
5. rights, privacy or attribution concerns.

Do not attach third-party video, player streams, proprietary source segments or
other material unless you have permission to redistribute it. Security issues
or sensitive rights enquiries may be sent privately to
`contact@wiregate26.com`.

Changes to published data must update `data/manifest.json`, pass the full
reproducibility workflow, and explain whether they correct source data, derived
output or documentation. Published releases are immutable; corrections receive
a new version and changelog entry.
